## PawPal+ System Classes (as of March 28, 2026)

- **Task**: Represents a single activity for a pet. Fields: description, time, frequency, completion status.
- **Pet**: Stores pet details and a list of tasks.
- **Owner**: Manages multiple pets and provides access to all their tasks.
- **Scheduler**: The "Brain" that retrieves, organizes, and manages tasks across pets.

**Goal:**
Keep the codebase focused on these four classes and their responsibilities. Avoid adding unrelated features or complexity.
